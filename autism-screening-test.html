About This Screening Tool

This free self-assessment questionnaire is designed to help you explore whether you might be on the autism spectrum and whether professional evaluation could be beneficial.

Important: This tool is a screening questionnaire, not a diagnostic assessment. Only a qualified healthcare professional can provide an autism diagnosis.

What to Expect

• You'll answer 10 questions about traits commonly associated with autism

• Takes about 5-10 minutes to complete

• Get a screening score and personalized recommendations

• Find evaluation resources in your area

Privacy: Your information is used only for resources. We don't sell or share your data.

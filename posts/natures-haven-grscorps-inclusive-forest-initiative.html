GRSCORP Partners with Saratoga Land Management Corporation for Inclusive Forest Land Project

Global Relief and Support Corporation (GRSCORP.US) is thrilled to announce a transformative partnership with Saratoga Land Management Corporation. Richard St. Armand, Head Forester at Saratoga Land Management Corp.saratogaland.com , and the GRSCORP board have officially signed a contract for forest land planning on the newly acquired 101-acre property in Delanson, NY 12053.
Creating a Haven for Individuals with Autism

This strategic acquisition aligns seamlessly with GRSCORP’s mission and purpose. The organization envisions developing an inclusive nature trail and outdoor activities tailored specifically for individuals with autism. This thoughtfully designed space will be open to the public and will feature:

  • Enhanced special needs assistance
  • No queues for individuals with autism
  • Therapy animals
  • A family-friendly camping area
Supporting the Mission

Funds raised and profits generated from this project will be reinvested into GRSCORP’s advocacy, education, and support services for individuals with autism and their families. This initiative represents a beacon of hope, offering a nurturing environment where families and individuals impacted by autism can connect with nature and one another.

Future Plans

Initial developments include access to the land on both sides of the highway, the creation of a camping area, a range, and a nature trail within the forest. Additional plans will be outlined during the course of the project, ensuring the land’s full potential is realized.

This partnership underscores GRSCORP’s commitment to fostering inclusive, supportive, and enriching environments for individuals with autism and their families. Stay tuned for updates as this exciting project progresses.

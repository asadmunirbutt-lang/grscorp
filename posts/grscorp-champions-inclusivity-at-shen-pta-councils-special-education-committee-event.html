GRSCorp Champions Inclusivity at Shen PTA Council’s Special Education Committee Event

📅 2024-02-07 📚 GRSCorp Blog ✨ Autism Resources

GRSCorp Advocates for Inclusive Communities

We’re delighted to share the success of the recent event organized by the Shen PTA Council Special Education Committee. At GRSCorp, we’re dedicated to volunteering and advocating for the rights of individuals with special needs. It’s heartening to witness the community’s collective efforts in making this event a beacon of inclusivity.

Appreciating Shen PTA Council, Special Educators, and the Community

A big shoutout to the Shen PTA Council, special educators, and the entire community for joining hands to make this event a resounding success. Your commitment to creating inclusive spaces resonates with our mission, and we’re proud to be part of such a vibrant community.

Collaboration with Inspiring Organizations

The event saw the participation of Academicians, parents, teachers, and PTA members. We extend our gratitude to the supporting organizations:

  • Shenendehowa School District Unified Sports proudly fosters inclusivity and camaraderie, uniting students of all abilities through a shared passion for sports and promoting the spirit of teamwork, respect, and friendship. Information regarding the program was shared by the coach (S).

 

 

  • Bring On The Spectrum: 0Bring on the Spectrum, empowers neurodiverse individuals in the Capital Region by fostering their best quality of life through inclusive social, recreational, and lifestyle activities for all ages and abilities.

 

 

 

  • Saratoga Sensory: Saratoga Sensory, led by a  Sensory Integration Certified OT with expertise in Ayres Sensory Integration, neurobiology, and family-centered intervention, offers personalized support for sensory-related disorders, feeding difficulties, and behavior challenges, providing tailored solutions for you and your child.

 

 

 

  • STEP, spearheaded by Executive Director Karen Stanley-White, is a pioneering organization dedicated to the transformative power of equine-assisted therapy, specializing in support for individuals with Multiple Sclerosis and led by a committed team of professionals in the field.

 

 

 

                                                                        GRSCorp Stands Strong for Inclusivity

At GRSCorp, we stand unwavering in our commitment to fostering inclusivity and social responsibility. The collaboration with organizations like Shen PTA Council, Bring On The Spectrum, Saratoga Sensory, THS, and Shen Unified Sports motivates us to continue advocating for the rights of individuals with special needs.

Together, We Make a Real Difference!

In a world where every effort counts, the collective impact of events like these reminds us that together, we can create a more inclusive and supportive environment for everyone. Cheers to unity, diversity, and making the world a better place!

For more updates on our advocacy and community initiatives, stay tuned to the GRSCorp website. grscorp.us

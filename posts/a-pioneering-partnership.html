Global Relief and Support Corporation and The People’s Real Estate Group Join Forces for a Cause

Date: October 13, 2023

In a remarkable event that unfolded in Latham, New York, on October 13, 2023, The People’s Real Estate Group and GRS CORP joined hands in a historic Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) signing. This partnership brings together two entities from different sectors, both with a strong commitment to making a positive impact.

A Vision of Empowerment and Inclusion

Represented by Hassan Khan, the Broker/Owner of The People’s Real Estate Group, the team pledged to give back to the community in a meaningful way. To be more specific, The People’s Real Estate Group, Top 100 Real Estate brokerages in the Capital Region, pledged to offer a discount on their commissions for individuals and families with special needs when buying or selling real estate. But that’s not all. In addition, the real commitment lies in their promise to donate 20% of their commissions from these transactions to GRS CORP.

Asad Butt, Chairman of the Board at GRS CORP, was present at the event to represent the corporation. The core mission of GRS CORP is to empower individuals with autism to lead fulfilling lives. This mission extends to fostering a culture of acceptance, understanding, and inclusion in diverse communities. Through comprehensive support, advocacy, education, and enrichment programs, they tirelessly strive to unlock the full potential of each individual. In doing so, they ensure their safety, respect, and active participation in society. MoU also includes awareness events organized by GRS Corp for the real estate agents of The People’s Real Estate Group on educating the needs of families and individuals with special needs.

A Win-Win Partnership

This partnership is a win-win for all parties involved. For individuals and families with special needs in the real estate market, this collaboration brings immediate financial relief. It effectively makes their real estate transactions more accessible and stress-free. On the other hand, GRS CORP benefits significantly from The People’s Real Estate Group’s generous donations. These donations serve to further their mission and extend their reach, broadening their impact.

The event marked a significant step towards fostering a more inclusive and caring society. By choosing The People’s Real Estate Group as their trusted partner in real estate, clients will not only secure top-notch service but also directly contribute to a worthy cause that has a meaningful impact.

The MoU signing was a poignant reminder that businesses can indeed make a substantial difference in the community when they join forces. This collaboration is a testament to the positive change that can happen when organizations come together for a common purpose and work in harmony.

We look forward to witnessing the positive impact this partnership will bring to the lives of individuals with special needs in our community. As this inspiring collaboration unfolds, it serves as a beacon of hope, clearly demonstrating that businesses can be powerful vehicles for change, especially when they align their missions and efforts.

Together, The People’s Real Estate Group and GRS CORP are creating a brighter, more inclusive future for everyone.

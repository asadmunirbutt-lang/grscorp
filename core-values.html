Core Values

Vision Statement

“Creating a world where individuals with autism thrive, reach their full potential, and are embraced as valued members of diverse and inclusive communities, fostering empowerment, understanding, and acceptance for all. We envision a future where empathy and inclusion are the cornerstones of our collective journey toward a more equitable and compassionate world for everyone.”

Mission Statement

“Our mission is to empower individuals with autism to lead fulfilling lives, while fostering a culture of acceptance, understanding, and inclusion in diverse communities. Through comprehensive support, advocacy, education, and enrichment programs, we strive to unlock the full potential of each individual, ensuring their safety, respect, and active participation in society.”

Purpose

The Global Relief & Support Corporation (GRSCorp) exists to enhance the lives of people and families impacted by autism. Our goal is to enable individuals on the Autism Spectrum to lead independent, dignified lives while being valued for their unique abilities. GRS Corp achieves this through:

  • Advocacy: Championing autism rights and needs at various levels.
  • Education: Offering resources to boost understanding and acceptance of autism.
  • Support Services: Connecting families with vital resources and networks.
  • Community: Creating a supportive community through networking and events.
  • Nature Programs: Exploring outdoor activities for the benefit of those with autism.
  • School Collaboration: Partnering with schools for better support and awareness.
  • Affiliates Network: Establishing a nationwide network for local, state, and national support.
    In short, GRS Corp strives to provide a nurturing environment for individuals with autism, enabling them to thrive and lead meaningful lives.

Goals

Global Relief & Support Corporation (GRSCorp)
  • Raise Awareness: Increase public understanding and awareness of autism by organizing awareness campaigns and educational events.
  • Advocacy Success: Successfully network and advocate for policy changes and legal measures that promote the rights and well-being of individuals with autism.
  • Resource Expansion: Develop and curate a comprehensive set of educational resources for families, educators, and communities, available both online and offline.
  • Support Network: Expand the reach of support services, ensuring that individuals and families across various regions have access to appropriate resources and networks.
  • Inclusive Community: Host regular community-building events and initiatives that bring together individuals with autism and their families, fostering a sense of belonging.
  • Nature Integration: Establish and promote nature-based programs that provide therapeutic and recreational benefits to individuals on the Autism Spectrum.
  • Educational Partnerships: Collaborate with schools to establish Special Education Parents Teachers Associations (SEPTA) and facilitate workshops that enhance understanding of autism among educators.
  • Affiliate Network Growth: Successfully establish a network of local, state, and national affiliates to amplify the organization’s impact and reach.
  • Empowerment Programs: Develop programs and workshops that equip individuals with autism with practical skills to enhance their independence and self-esteem.
  • Research Contribution: Partner with research institutions to contribute to the understanding of autism and advocate for more inclusive research practices.
  • Technology Utilization: Leverage technology to create digital platforms for information sharing, online support groups, and virtual events.
  • Collaborative Initiatives: Forge partnerships with other organizations, both within and outside the autism community, to create a more inclusive society overall.

Our commitment to improving the lives of individuals with autism by advocating for their rights, providing educational resources, fostering a supportive community, and exploring innovative approaches to enrich their experiences.
